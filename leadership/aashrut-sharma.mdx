## Current Venture: Superadmission

### Building India's Admission Infrastructure

**Role:** Co-Founder & CEO\
**Status:** DPIIT-Recognized, MSME Certified, actively building with government alignment

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The Problem We're Addressing" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    India's 35 million annual higher education applicants navigate a fragmented system with 150\+ examination bodies and 200\+ counseling portals. This fragmentation leads to:

    - 40-50% of students making preventable errors (missed deadlines, incorrect forms)
    - ₹15,000 crore annual cost in direct and indirect inefficiencies
    - No unified infrastructure layer (unlike payments or identity systems)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Our Approach: Four-Layer DPI" icon="layer-group">
    Building infrastructure, not just another platform:

    1. **Identity & Verification:** APAAR ID \+ DigiLocker integration
    2. **Intelligent Matching:** Gale-Shapley Algorithm for stable allocation
    3. **Real-Time Transparency:** Live vacancy tracking and probability estimation
    4. **Unified Settlement:** UPI \+ Academic Bank of Credits integration

    Positioned as neutral public infrastructure, comparable to UPI or Aadhaar.
  </Accordion>

## Journey to Superadmission

### From Observation to Infrastructure

**Summer 2024: Problem Recognition**\
During B.Tech admission counseling season, identified systematic inefficiencies affecting students across Delhi. Built initial service (CollegeCult) to help 143\+ students navigate the process.

**Late 2024: Strategic Pivot**\
Recognized that counseling services treat symptoms, not root causes. Made the decision to pivot from service-layer to infrastructure-layer solution.

**Q4 2024-Q1 2025: Formalization**

- Incorporated as Drox Consultancy Pvt. Ltd.
