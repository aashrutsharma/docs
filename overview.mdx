## Building Digital Public Infrastructure at 21

Aashrut Sharma and Unnati Vijay are co-founding Superadmission to transform India's higher education access through Digital Public Infrastructure. At 20-21 years old, they're not building a typical startup—they're building nation-scale reform infrastructure.

## Shared Journey

Both are current students at Faculty of Technology, Delhi University, balancing rigorous academic commitments with building national-scale infrastructure. Their partnership emerged from firsthand observation of systematic inefficiencies in India's admission process during the 2024 counseling season.

**What makes this partnership work:**

- **Complementary strengths:** Strategic vision (Aashrut) paired with operational execution (Unnati)
- **Shared values:** Public infrastructure philosophy over private platform capture
- **Long-term commitment:** Building for decades of impact, not quarterly metrics
- **Academic grounding:** Engineering education informing technical architecture decisions
