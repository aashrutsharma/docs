## Shared Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Summer 2024: CollegeCult Pilot">
    Co-founded CollegeCult to address admission counseling inefficiencies. Served 143+ Delhi students, but recognized counseling only addressed symptoms—not root causes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Late 2024: Strategic Pivot">
    Made the difficult decision to pivot from counseling services to infrastructure. Chose long-term systemic impact over short-term revenue.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Q4 2024-Q1 2025: Validation Phase">
    Incorporated Drox Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. Secured pre-seed backing from invyrVentures. Achieved DPIIT recognition and MSME certification. Reached Zerodha WTFund Round 4/5.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Q1 2025: National Recognition">
    Showcasing at Startup Mahakumbh 2025. Registered for India AI Impact Summit 2025. Presented vision to InfoEdge founder Sanjeev Bikhchandani.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Q2 2025 & Beyond: DPI Deployment">
    Moving toward pilot programs with government stakeholders. Aligning with NEP 2020 implementation. Building India's first unified admission infrastructure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Building for FYLS Dubai 2026

Both founders are committed to attending the Future Youth Leadership Summit to:

1. **Learn Global DPI Best Practices**: How other nations have built education infrastructure (UK's UCAS, US's CommonApp)
2. **Share India's DPI Innovation**: Position India's UPI-Aadhaar-DigiLocker stack as global model for public infrastructure
3. **Build Cross-Border Networks**: Leverage AFS and nonprofit experience to create lasting international relationships
4. **Accelerate Superadmission**: Bring insights on stakeholder diplomacy, policy advocacy, and ethical tech back to India's ecosystem
