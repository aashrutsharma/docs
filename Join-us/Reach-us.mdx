## For pilot and institutional inquiries

If you are a counselling authority, state education department, or government official interested in exploring a pilot or understanding the platform's policy alignment, reach Unnati directly.

**Unnati Vijay — Co-Founder and COO**
unnati@superadmission.in

She leads institutional partnerships and government relations. She will give you a direct, specific answer about what a pilot involves, what it requires from your side, and what it produces.

**Aashrut Sharma — Co-Founder**

## For hiring and joining

See the Open Roles page. Send a note to office@superadmission.in with the role name in the subject line.

No templates. No formatted cover letters. Just tell us what you have done and why this problem interests you.

---

## For press and research

If you are a journalist, researcher, or policy analyst interested in India's admissions infrastructure or Superadmission specifically, the Blueprint is the right starting point. It is written to be a primary resource.

For interview requests or research collaboration, write to office@superadmission.in.
