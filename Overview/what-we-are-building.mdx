Superadmission is India's first unified admissions infrastructure: a shared digital layer, built on Aadhaar, DigiLocker, APAAR, and UPI, that sits beneath the country's 200\+ independent counselling systems and coordinates what they currently cannot coordinate on their own.

The prototype is approximately 85% complete. This page describes what exists today.

---

## Core systems — live

**Identity and verification layer** Students register once using Aadhaar OTP authentication. Documents — marksheets, caste certificates, income certificates, exam scores — are fetched directly from issuing authorities via DigiLocker. The student uploads nothing. The verification result travels across every integrated counselling.

**Seat allocation engine** Built on the Gale-Shapley deferred acceptance algorithm. Configurable per counselling authority — each authority defines their own seat matrix, category rules, and round structure. Validated against JoSAA 2023 and 2024 public outcomes. Produces identical results.

**Pravesh AI** The guidance and decision-support layer. Surfaces allocation probability by rank and category. Explains seat offers in plain language. Provides round-specific trend insights. Flags document gaps before they become rejections. For students without external guidance, this is the layer that levels the playing field.

**Virtual Spot Round** A live, rank-gated digital environment for end-of-cycle seat selection. Students see their queue position in real time, see available seats by branch and category with previous-year closing data, and make decisions with an active countdown timer. No travel. No demand drafts. No physical queues.

**Audit trail** Every system event — document verified, choice saved, seat allocated, payment confirmed — generates a unique cryptographic hash. Every event is source-attributed. Every allocation decision is traceable step by step. This is not a log. It is a permanent, tamper-evident record of every consequential action in the admissions journey.

**Payments** All fee transactions across counsellings run through UPI/NPCI. Unified payments dashboard. Due dates surfaced before they arrive. Refund status tracked automatically. Every payment hash-stamped.

## What comes next

Reserved seat automatic reallocation and regional language support are in active development. The first live pilot — integration with a state counselling authority for a defined cohort and round — is the immediate priority.

The platform is not claimed to be complete. The systems above are real and demonstrable. A pilot is where the architecture meets real load conditions, real student data, and real counselling timelines — and gets validated or refined.
